Next-Gen

Recognizing Up-and-Coming Content Creators

Next-Gen: Recognizing Up-and-Coming Content Creators is an awards platform dedicated to celebrating and elevating the rising stars of content creating over the past year. As one of the first systems focused specifically on emerging talent, we aim to shine a spotlight on creators who are shaping the future of online entertainment.

Our mission is to honor content creators who not only produce engaging and original content but also build vibrant, welcoming communities that connect people around the world. We track trends, understand the pulse of the content-creating world, and feature awards per categories covering a wide range of influence, content styles, and community experiences.

Why does this matter? Because it's you — the viewers and supporters — who have the power to nominate and select the winners. Your voice decides who gets recognized for their creativity, dedication, and impact. As we, creators believe that you — supporters — know exactly who ultimately deserves these recognitions.

The Next-Gen Streamer Awards 2025 will be held across multiple conventions, giving fans the chance to experience the excitement live and in person. Join us as your favorite streamers walk the red carpet, take part in live interviews, enjoy special performances, and celebrate the awards ceremony up close.
